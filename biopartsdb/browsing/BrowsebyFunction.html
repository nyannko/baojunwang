Browse by Function

  • Hardwires

    Parts whose presence in a gene define expression levels directly.

  • Input

    Modulators of gene expression based on external inputs, in the form of small molecules, light, or metals.

  • Buffer Gates

    Parts activated by regulators, with ascending response functions.

  • NOT Gates

    Parts repressed by regulators, with descending response functions.

  • AND Gates

    Systems with separately-expressed parts that won't work independently.

  • Insulation

    Sequences with no regulation or change to expression levels, but serve to isolate parts' funcions from genetic context, improving modularity.

  • Vectors

    Components of the backbone surrounding a circuit. Could also function as a hardwire.

  • Communication

    Regulation between cells, based on quorum sensing autoinducers.

  • Memory

    Parts for systems where a temporary input can set a constant memory output.

  • Output

    Endpoints of a circuit. Normally reporters or systems that regulate a gene of interest.
