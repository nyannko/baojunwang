Riboregulators


RNA structures designed to modulate transcription or translation based on a linear trans-RNA.

All riboregulators on the list use a fluorescein standard to measure activity, so dynamic ranges and relations between parts on the same experiment should be fairly reliable. However, measurements taken from different experiments shouldn't be compared.
