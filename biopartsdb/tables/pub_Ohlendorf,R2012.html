Ohlendorf, R., Vidavski, R. R., Eldar, A., Moffat, K. & Möglich, A. From dusk till dawn: One-plasmid systems for light-regulated gene expression. J. Mol. Biol. 416, 534–542 (2012).

In this publication, Ohlendorf and colleagues describe the construction of plasmids for blue light detection (pDusk and pDawn), induced by blue light, utilizing the two-component system YF1/FixJ. Although the dynamic range reported here is comparatively small, by using an inverter with the cI repressor, a blue-light inducible system could be developed with dyamic ranges over 500-fold.
