In this publication, Ramakrishnan and colleagues characterize an ultraviolet light-inducible two component UirS/UirR system. Although the regulators expressions have been optimized and hardwired with constitutive promoters, the circuit only reach modest dynamic ranges. The system is still a relevant addition to the Tabor lab's range of optogenetic promoters.
