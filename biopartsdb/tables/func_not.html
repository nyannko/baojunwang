NOT Gates


Parts repressed by regulators, with descending response functions, so a high ON input inverts to a low OFF signal. High and low expression values were shown where possible. Two types of repression are represented, either by DNA binding proteins or trans-interacting RNA. On most publications, there were more parts than reported here. Only the ones with the highest orthogonality and dynamic range are in the database.
