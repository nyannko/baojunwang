In this publication, Ong and colleagues design and characterize a near-infrared light inducible circuit based on the two-component system bphP1/PpsR2. Although the regulators expressions were optimized and hardwired to constitutive promoters, the system only reached modest dynamic ranges. It is still a welcome addition to the light sensors characterized by the Tabor Lab.
