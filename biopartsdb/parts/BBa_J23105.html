Description

Part of the widely-used Anderson promoters collection, characterized by Prof. Christopher Anderson and the 2006 Berkley iGEM team, which derived 20 promoters from mutating BBaJ23119. The data used here was taken directly from the iGEM repository, although it lacks in detail when disclosing measurement/culture conditions.
